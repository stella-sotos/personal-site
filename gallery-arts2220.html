Arts 2220 Project on the Overconsumption of Meat


With this project I wanted to discuss the overconsumption of meat in America and the repercussions on the environment. I ended up focusing specifically on cows since they are a prominent symbol of animal agriculture and since the negative impact of consuming beef is so extreme.

I featured many vintage advertisements for meat, demonstrating America's long running attachment to and nostalgia for these products. The repetition in my imagery is used to showcase the scale of this issue. The landscapes I featured are faded and depleted as a result of this phenomenon.

This was one of my very first projects using Adobe Photoshop. I am proud of how these images turned out and of the clarity of my message.
